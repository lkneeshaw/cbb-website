Skate with us

We are roller derby skaters who are changing this sport and rocking the skating world. We train to win by practicing, cross-training, testing and scrimmaging regularly. We value athleticism, camaraderie and acceptance. You’re not just joining a team—you’re joining a community.

Contact Our Coaches

How to join

  1. Send us an email.
  2. Sit in on a practice and decide whether or not you want to join.
  3. Complete league documents.
  4. Pay insurance and first month of dues.
  5. Pass the WFTDA skills test and you're ready to bout!

Referees: Skating Officials

Our zebras. If you can skate, you can be in the middle of the action. Referees help track points, call penalties, and keep our players safe. Contact us for more information.

Become A Referee

NSOs: Non-Skating Officials

Our ninjas. NSOs are the unsung heros of roller derby. We can’t have a bout without them. NSOs track points, penalties, and other stats. No skates required.
